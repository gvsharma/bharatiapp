Welcome to Bharati Milk Products

In the heart of the bustling coastal city of Vizag, Andhra Pradesh, India, Bharati Milk Products stands as a beacon of quality and authenticity in the dairy industry. With a legacy of delivering goodness in the form of milk, curd, paneer, ghee, butter, and more, Bharati Milk Products has become a household name synonymous with wholesome dairy products.

The Local Choice with a Global Reach

Founded in the vibrant city of Vizag, Bharati Milk Products has grown from a local dairy farm to a renowned provider of dairy delights that cater not only to the families and individuals of Vizag but also extend their reach to a 100-kilometer radius around the city. With a dedication to sourcing, processing, and delivering dairy products of the highest quality, Bharati Milk Products has earned the trust of its customers far and wide.
